What is a period and why do we have them?

A period, also known as a menstrual cycle is when the tissue from your uterus comes out

of your vagina. Women get their period because every month their body prepares for an egg

to be fertilized. If it does not get fertilized then there is no use for it and then it

will be released.

Around what age do I get my period?

Normally girls will get their first period around the ages of 11 - 14 and it is totally

ok to tell your mom about if since she will know what to do.

Is it normal for my stomach to hurt when I am on my period?

Yes, is it normal if your stomach hurts. It is usually just cramps and it happens because

your uterus is contracting to shed the inner tissue.

How can I manage my period pain?

Some ways to ease your pain is to take over-the-counter medication (i.e Advil or

Tylenol), get a hot water bottle and place it on your stomach, take a hot bath, or exercise.

What does it mean when your period does not come monthly?

For girls that just had their periods, it is alright if you do not get it monthly. Some

things that might make your period stop are birth controls, over exercising, diet, stress, emotional, or hormonal problems.

Who should I tell about my first period?

Tell anyone that you are close with and comfortable with about your situation. If you are

confused, you can always ask someone to help you out, tell you the best brands for pads or tampons, and how you how to put it on.

What is self care?

Self care is the action that an individual takes to care for their mental, physical, and emotional needs.

What is Self Defense?

Self Defense is a concept that let people learn certain techniques to help them escape or

prevent certain situations, assaults, amd make the individual more aware of their surroundings.

Can Self Defense help me?

Absolutely, you might not successfully perfect your skills but learning self defense can

help slow down the attacker or increase your chances of getting away.

them. Call the police, that might be the safest option.
